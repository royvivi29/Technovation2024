Rule & Regulation

(1).Preliminary round- It will have multiple sets of rounds and in each set 5 groups will be participating out of which top 2 best performing teams will be advancing to the next round.

(2).Semi final- Teams reaching to semi finals will be having a rapid fire round in which 20 questions will be asked in 2 mins time stamp to all the teams individually. Top 5 teams will be advancing to the final round.

(3).Final round- Quiz will be a mix of individual questions to each group, pictorial round, rapid fire,

If any group fails to answer it will to passed down to next group.

For individual questions each correct answer carries 5 points.

Pictorial round each correct answer carries 3 points.

Rapid fire each correct answer carry 2 point.

Questions cannot be passed in rapid fire round and for individual questions and

pictorial round each passed question carries 1 point.
