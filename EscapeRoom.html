Rule & Regulation

Get ready to put your mathematical skills to the test in our thrilling Escape Room Math Challenge! You and your team of three students will be locked in a room filled with mind-bending mathematical puzzles and riddles. The clock is ticking, and the first team to successfully solve all the problems and escape the room will emerge as the champions.

It is a group (at most 3 members) event, with one leader

Preliminary Round

• Leader represents the team in the preliminary round (mathematical quiz test).

• Top 10 teams progress to the final round

Final Round:

• Consists of 5 math stations.

• Solve puzzles to escape with minimum time and maximum correct answers.

Prize and Recognition:

• Top 3-performing teams receive prizes and recognition.
