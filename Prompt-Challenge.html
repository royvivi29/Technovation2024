Introduction:

Welcome to the exciting world of the Prompt Writing Challenge, a thrilling event during TechFeast-24 that promises not only a test of your creative prowess but also a journey of fun-filled learning!

Are you ready to embark on a writing adventure that combines ingenuity, wit, and a dash of unpredictability? This challenge is not just about words; it's about the art of crafting prompts that spark inspiration and creativity. Picture it as a playground where your imagination takes center stage, and each prompt is a new swing to propel you into uncharted territories of thought.

In this event, we're not just challenging your writing skills; we're encouraging you to think outside the box, dive into the depths of your creativity, and emerge with prompts that leave a lasting impression. The beauty lies not just in the words you choose, but in the worlds you create with them.

But wait, it's not all serious business! Fun is our co-pilot in this journey. We believe that learning is most effective when accompanied by laughter and enjoyment. So, gear up for an event that not only hones your writing abilities but also guarantees a good time.

Remember, this is more than a challenge; it's an opportunity to explore, experiment, and revel in the joy of crafting prompts that not only entertain but also inspire. Let the fun learning begin, and may your prompts be as vibrant and dynamic as the imaginative mind behind them!

Participant Requirement: Mobile with internet

No of Rounds: Three

Round-1: All participants will write prompts for given task by Jury.

Evaluation: (Top 10 participants will be shortlisted for next round)

Round-2: All participants will be given a story writing task to complete, prompts are evaluated by jury

Evaluation: (Top 6 participants will be shortlisted for next round)

Round-3: All participants will be given a Technical activity to complete, prompts are evaluated by jury.

Evaluation: (Top 3 participants will be shortlisted for next round)
